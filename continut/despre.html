Formatul obișnuit al unui Grand Prix:

  1. FP1 - Free Practice 1 - 60 minute
  2. FP2 - Free Practice 2 - 60 minute
  3. FP3 - Free Practice 3 - 60 minute
  4. Q - Qualifying:
    • Q1 - 18 minute
    • Q2 - 15 minute
    • Q3 - 12 minute
  5. R - Race - 305 kilometeri sau 2 ore + 1 tur
